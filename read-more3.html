Agrotein Meal Booster

Agrotein Meal Booster

It contains natural source of Phosphorous and Calcium for the plants.

It can be easily degradable by soil micro-organisms.

Since rich in Phosphorous, it induces more white roots to plants /crop.

It helps uptake of maximum nutrients to plants / roots.

It helps enhance plant growth, induce flowering, increasing fruits bearing, fruit weight, fruit size and fruits colour.

Being rich in Calcium, it prevents fruits cracking during maturity.
